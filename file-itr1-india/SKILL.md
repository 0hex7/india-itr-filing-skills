# File ITR-1 (India) — salary-only

> Tax figures below are **AY 2026-27 (FY 2025-26)**. Re-verify the slabs, 87A rebate, standard deduction, and due dates each year on incometax.gov.in — the *process* is stable, the *numbers* change.

## Who can use ITR-1 (Sahaj)
Resident & ordinarily resident, **total income ≤ ₹50 lakh**, income only from **salary/pension**, **one house property**, and **other sources** (interest/dividend); agricultural income ≤ ₹5,000.

**NOT eligible → use ITR-2/ITR-3:** any business/professional income, **capital gains** (any share/MF/property/gold sale), **intraday or F&O trading**, **foreign assets or foreign income** (ESOPs, US stocks, overseas account), company director, unlisted shares, income > ₹50 lakh.

## 1 — Collect & reconcile
- **Form 16** (employer) · **AIS + TIS** (portal → AIS; PDF password = PAN-lowercase + DDMMYYYY DOB) · **Form 26AS** (e-File → Income Tax Returns → View 26AS) · **bank interest** (savings + FD).
- **Reconcile 26AS = AIS = Form 16** for salary + TDS.
- **Total ALL interest from AIS** — it lists interest across every bank; people routinely miss interest from a second/third bank.

## 2 — Regime & tax (New Regime = default)
- **Standard deduction ₹75,000** (auto).
- **Section 87A rebate**: total income ≤ **₹12,00,000** → tax **₹0** (rebate up to ₹60,000). With the ₹75k SD, salary up to **₹12.75 lakh → ₹0 tax**.
- Do **NOT** opt out of the new regime unless the old regime is genuinely cheaper (rare for salary-only — old regime needs big 80C/HRA to beat it).
- No HRA/LTA/80C/80D under the new regime.

## 3 — File on the portal
1. incometax.gov.in → **Login** (User ID = PAN).
2. **e-File → Income Tax Returns → File Income Tax Return**.
3. AY **2026-27** → **Online** → **Individual** → **ITR-1**.
4. Reason for filing → **"Taxable income is more than the basic exemption limit."** (True whenever income > ₹4L — the 87A rebate zeroes the *tax*, not the *income*.)
5. Wizard: opt out of new regime → **No**; salary exemptions → **No**.
6. Verify pre-filled: personal info; **gross salary** (= Form 16/26AS); **standard deduction ₹75,000**; add **all interest** (Schedule OS).
7. Confirm **tax = ₹0** (or pay any balance due).
8. **Bank account** → ensure at least one is **pre-validated**.
9. **Preview → Submit → e-Verify (Aadhaar OTP)** to the registered mobile — **within 30 days** or the return is invalid.

## Checklist
- [ ] 26AS / AIS / Form 16 reconciled; **all** bank interest added
- [ ] New regime kept; SD ₹75,000; 87A → tax ₹0 (if income ≤ ₹12L)
- [ ] Bank account pre-validated
- [ ] Filed by **31 July** (ITR-1 due date); **e-verified within 30 days**

## Common mistakes
- Missing interest from a secondary bank (AIS shows them all).
- Assuming "₹0 tax means below the exemption limit" → still pick the "income above basic exemption" filing reason.
- Not e-verifying (unverified return = not filed).
- Using ITR-1 despite a capital gain or a foreign asset → you must move to ITR-2/ITR-3.
